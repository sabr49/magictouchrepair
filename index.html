The Leader in Glass Scratch Repair

Located in the Baltimore/Washington area, Magic Touch has revolutionized the scratch glass repair industry.

With our unique and proprietary process, we have perfected the ability to remove scratches from glass in a distortion-free manner.

Scratches in curtain wall systems, french doors, palladian windows, and mirrors all can be repaired at a fraction of replacement cost. We have worked on glass in a variety of situations including commercial buildings, condominiums and residential homes. We have repaired antique automobile glass and worked on luxury yachts.
